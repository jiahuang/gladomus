Getting directions
Text "mapd s.[starting location] e.[ending location]" without the "[]" in order to get driving directions
Text "mapp s.[starting location] e.[ending location] d.[departure time]" in order to get public transit directions. Want to look it up by arrival time? just switch the "d" with an "a"
Looking something up on Wikipedia
Text "wiki a.[article]" to get a summary of the article.
Want look at the rest of the article? Text "wiki a.[article] s.toc" to see the table of contents.
Interested in a particular section? Text "wiki a.[article] s.[section number or section title]"
